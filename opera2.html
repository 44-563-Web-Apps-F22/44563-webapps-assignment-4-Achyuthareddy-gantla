The Gondoliers
ACT I The Piazzetta, Venice

ACT I Scene.— the Piazzetta, Venice. The Ducal Palace on the right. Fiametta, Giulia, Vittoria, and other Contadine discovered, each tying a bouquet of roses.


(During this chorus Antonio, Francesco, Giorgio, and other Gondoliers have entered unobserved by the Girls—at first two, then two more, then four, then half a dozen, then the remainder of the Chorus.)

FRANC

Good morrow, pretty maids; for whom prepare ye These floral tributes extraordinary?

FIA

For Marco and Giuseppe Palmieri, The pink and flower of all the Gondolieri.
